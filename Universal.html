The Sweetest surprise

Once upon a rainy afternoon, little Maya stood by the window, bored and restless. Her grandma noticed and smiled.

"Want to help me bake some cookies?" she asked.

Maya's eyes lit up. "Chocolate chip?" "Of course," Grandma winked.

As the smell of warm cookies filled the kitchen, Maya giggled, sneaking chocolate chips when Grandma wasn’t looking. They shaped the dough, talked about school, dreams, and silly things.

When the cookies were done, Maya took a bite. “These are magical,” she said with crumbs on her lips.

Grandma laughed. “It’s not just the chocolate. It’s the love we put in.”

And from that day on, rainy days were no longer boring — they were cookie days.
