Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) white sugar
  • 1 cup (220g) brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (340g) chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Line baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Combine dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt) in a separate bowl.
  5. Mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture gradually.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto trays (about 1–2 tbsp per cookie).
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes until edges are golden.
  9. Cool on tray for a few minutes, then transfer to wire rack.

Notes:

  • Chill dough for 30 mins if cookies spread too much.
  • Use half chocolate chips and half chopped chocolate for gooey texture.
  • Slightly underbake for soft cookies; bake longer for crispier ones.
  • Store in airtight container for 4–5 days.
